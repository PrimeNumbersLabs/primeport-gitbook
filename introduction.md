# Introduction

## PrimePort: The Multichain NFT Marketplace

PrimePort is the NFT marketplace built by [Prime Numbers Labs](https://primenumbers.xyz). Originally launched on the XDC Network, PrimePort has expanded to support **Base** (Ethereum L2), bringing multichain NFT trading, staking, and exploration to a wider audience.

Whether you are a creator launching a collection, a collector looking for unique digital art, or a staker seeking yield from PRFI ONFTs, PrimePort provides the tools you need.

***

### Why PrimePort Stands Out

1. **Multichain Support** — PrimePort operates on both the XDC Network and Base. Users can trade and interact with NFTs across supported chains from a single interface.

2. **NFT Staking** — PRFI ONFTs on Base are yield-bearing vaults. Stake $PRFI inside your NFT to earn a share of the monthly reward pool, marketplace royalties, and [PrimeFi](https://app.primefi.xyz) profits. [Learn more →](prfi-onfts/README.md)

3. **Full-Featured Marketplace** — Fixed-price listings, timed auctions, offers, and a drops system for launching collections. All built in.

4. **Low Transaction Costs** — Near-zero gas fees on XDC and low L2 fees on Base mean you keep more of what you earn.

5. **Creator Tools** — Deploy smart contracts, mint single or batch NFTs, set royalties, and run drops with whitelists and scheduling — all from the PrimePort interface.

6. **Secure and Transparent** — Every transaction is recorded on-chain. Smart contracts are verified and auditable. Wallet connections use industry-standard protocols (WalletConnect, MetaMask).

### What You Can Do on PrimePort

**As a Collector**
- Browse collections on XDC and Base
- Buy NFTs at fixed prices or via auction
- Make offers on any NFT
- View detailed staking data, rarity, and ownership history

**As a Creator**
- Deploy your own smart contract directly from PrimePort
- Mint single NFTs or batch collections
- Launch drops with scheduling, whitelists, and minting waves
- Set and receive royalties on secondary sales

**As a Staker**
- Stake $PRFI inside your PRFI ONFT
- Level up to increase your multiplier and monthly rewards
- Merge two same-rarity NFTs into a higher-tier NFT
- Earn from marketplace royalties and PrimeFi profit sharing
